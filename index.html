Beyond Growth (BG) is a training and consultancy firm specialized in soft skills development, headquartered in Jakarta, Indonesia.

We are a global collective of business trainers with expertise in sales and leadership. ​ Our experience is shared in a practical way through result driven training programs.​  ​ As a global collective, Beyond Growth and ADD+ (The Netherlands), share a mutual belief and understanding ​on how to make businesses excel through impactful training.​ ​

Our Approach

Western knowledge with a local approach, understanding cultural differences.

VISION

To be a leading training collective impacting communication in the fields of sales and leadership within SEA region.

GOAL

The main goal of a BG training course is to improve the quality of communication. Our training modules are developed to change behaviour and increase confidence in (daily) decision making.

MISSION

To assist organizations and individuals in realizing their full commercial potential.
